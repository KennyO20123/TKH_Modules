Born and raised in The Bronx, I come from a backgroud where I knew education could play a pivital role in helping me achieve a better life. Not to say that The Bronx is all gloom and doom but where I grew up was. It was always instilled that the idea of what a "great career" was always revolved around that of a being doctor, lawyer etc. I first came across tech when I would build websites from scratch in elementary school, back then I learned mainly the fundamentals of HTML through my own research. Now that I know that this passion that I had (and still have) is such a coveted skill, I only see plenty of opportunities and hope in what I can consider as my "great career".
